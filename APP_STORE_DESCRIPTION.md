## Privacy & Permissions

### Permissions Required:
1. **Location Services (Optional)**
   - Purpose: "Scratch & Go uses your location to suggest nearby activities and local experiences tailored to your area."
   - When: First time user opens app or accesses location features
   - Can be denied: Yes, manual location entry available

2. **Calendar (Optional)**
   - Purpose: "Add activities to your calendar so you never forget a planned adventure."
   - When: User taps "Add to Calendar" on an activity
   - Can be denied: Yes, feature simply won't be available

### Data Collection:
- Activity preferences and filters
- Saved activities and ratings
- Subscription status
- Anonymous usage analytics (for app improvement)

### Data NOT Collected:
- Personal identifying information
- Contact information
- Payment details (handled by Apple/Google)
- Location tracking (only used when actively using app)
